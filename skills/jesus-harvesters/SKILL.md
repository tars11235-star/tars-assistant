# Jesus Harvesters Ministries

## About the Ministry

Jesus Harvesters Ministries (JHM) is a church-planting and discipleship ministry focused on:
- Evangelism
- Pastor training
- Medical mission trips
- Orphan care

Through 20+ years of work in East Africa, JHM has planted churches across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and South Sudan.
